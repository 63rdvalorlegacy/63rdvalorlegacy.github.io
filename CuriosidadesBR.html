Veterans After Service

Veterans of the 63rd Infantry can now schedule appointments online at Veteran and Pensioner Sections, facilitated by the integrated Corporate Army Database System, SIGA-SIPWEB, enhancing the process of granting benefits.

Veterans' Anthem

The anthem, known as the Expeditionary's Song, highlights the bravery, vigor, and victory of the war veterans. Here is an excerpt: "Do you know where I come from? / From the hill, the Engine, / From the jungles, the coffee plantations..."

Autism Pride Day

Also on June 18, Brazil celebrates Autism Pride Day, an initiative by the UK's Aspies for Freedom, to raise awareness of this neurodiversity within the autism spectrum.
